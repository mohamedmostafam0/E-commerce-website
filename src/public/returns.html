Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase! Learn about our simple and hassle-free return and exchange policy.

Our Return Policy

At BrandStore, we stand behind the quality of our products and want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not entirely happy, we're here to help with our hassle-free return and exchange policy.

Return Eligibility

Items are eligible for return or exchange if they meet the following criteria:

  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition
  • All original tags must still be attached
  • Items must be returned in their original packaging (if applicable)
  • You must have proof of purchase (order number, receipt, or confirmation email)

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Intimate apparel and swimwear for hygiene reasons
  • Face masks and personal care items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Gift cards
  • Items damaged through normal wear and tear or improper care

Quality Guarantee

If you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact our Customer Service team within 7 days of delivery. We'll arrange for a return or exchange at no cost to you.

How to Return or Exchange an Item

1

Initiate Your Return

Log into your BrandStore account, go to "Order History," find the order containing the item(s) you wish to return, and click "Return Items." If you checked out as a guest, use our Guest Return Portal and enter your order number and email address.

2

Complete the Return Form

Select the items you want to return, specify whether you want a refund or exchange, and provide the reason for your return. For exchanges, select the new size or color you'd like.

3

Print Your Return Label

Once your return is approved, we'll email you a prepaid return shipping label. Print the label and attach it to your package. If you don't have a printer, you can use our QR code option at partner shipping locations.

4

Package Your Return

Place the item(s) in the original packaging if possible, or a secure box or envelope. Include all original tags and the packing slip or a copy of your order confirmation.

5

Ship Your Return

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location. We recommend getting a tracking number for your records. International customers should use a trackable shipping method.

Expedited Exchanges

If you're requesting an exchange, we'll process it as quickly as possible. For popular items, we may ship your new size or color before we receive your return to ensure you get your exchange faster.

Refund Information

Refund Timeline

Here's what to expect once we receive your return:

  • Processing time: 3-5 business days for our team to inspect and process your return
  • Refund issuance: 1-2 business days to issue your refund once processed
  • Bank/credit card processing: 3-7 business days for the refund to appear on your statement (varies by financial institution)

Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase:

  • Credit/debit card purchases will be refunded to the same card
  • PayPal purchases will be refunded to your PayPal account
  • Store credit or gift card purchases will be refunded as store credit

Return Shipping Costs

Return shipping fees are handled as follows:

  • For standard returns, a flat fee of $5.99 will be deducted from your refund
  • Exchanges and returns due to defects or our errors are free (no shipping fee deducted)
  • International return shipping is the responsibility of the customer
  • Premium or BrandStore+ members enjoy free returns with no deductions

International Returns

For customers outside the United States, our return policy has a few additional considerations:

  • The return window is extended to 45 days to accommodate international shipping times
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer
  • We cannot refund any customs fees, import duties, or taxes paid on your original order
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping method for your return
  • Include your order number clearly on the outside of the package
