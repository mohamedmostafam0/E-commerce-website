Shopping

How do I place an order?
Placing an order is easy! Simply browse our catalog, select the items you'd like to purchase, choose your size and quantity, and click "Add to Cart." When you're ready to check out, click on the cart icon in the top right corner, review your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your shipping information and payment details to complete your purchase.
Do I need an account to shop?
While you can browse our products without an account, you'll need to create one to complete a purchase. Creating an account allows you to track your order, save your shipping information for future purchases, and receive exclusive offers. It only takes a minute to sign up!
How can I find my size?
We offer a comprehensive size guide for all our clothing items. You can find the size guide link on each product page or in the "Help" section of our website. Our size guides provide detailed measurements to help you find the perfect fit. If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up.
Can I modify or cancel my order after it's placed?
You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. To do so, log into your account, go to "Order History," find the order you'd like to modify, and click "Cancel Order." For modifications, please contact our customer service team immediately. After the 1-hour window, we begin processing orders, and cancellations or modifications may not be possible.

Shipping & Delivery

How much does shipping cost?
We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, a flat shipping fee of $5.99 applies. Express shipping is available for an additional charge and varies based on your location. International shipping rates vary by country and can be calculated at checkout.
How long will it take to receive my order?
Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days for delivery within the continental United States. Express shipping is available for 1-2 business day delivery. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days, depending on the destination and customs clearance. Please note that these are estimated delivery times and may vary due to factors beyond our control, such as weather or customs delays.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes upon arrival, which are the responsibility of the recipient and not included in our shipping charges.
How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history. For detailed tracking information, click on the tracking number provided or visit our "Track Order" page.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn, unwashed items in their original condition with tags attached. Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery. For full details, please visit our Returns & Exchanges page.
How do I return or exchange an item?
To initiate a return or exchange, log into your account, go to "Order History," find the order containing the item you want to return, and click "Return Items." Follow the instructions to complete the return process. You'll receive a return shipping label via email. For exchanges, indicate the new size or item you'd like in the return form.
Do I have to pay for return shipping?
Return shipping is free for exchanges or if you received a defective or incorrect item. For regular returns, a flat return shipping fee of $5.99 will be deducted from your refund. International returns are the responsibility of the customer unless the item received was defective or incorrect.
How long does it take to process a refund?
Once we receive your return, it typically takes 3-5 business days to process. After processing, refunds are issued to your original payment method and may take an additional 3-7 business days to appear on your statement, depending on your bank or credit card company.

Account & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. We also offer a secure "Buy Now, Pay Later" option through Affirm on orders over $50.
Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment processors to ensure your payment information is protected. We never store your full credit card details on our servers. Our website is SSL-certified, as indicated by the lock icon in your browser's address bar.
How do I reset my password?
If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Login" button, then select "Forgot Password." Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. For security reasons, the link expires after 24 hours.
Can I update my shipping address after placing an order?
You can update your shipping address within 1 hour of placing your order. After that, if your order hasn't shipped yet, please contact our customer service team immediately, and we'll do our best to accommodate the change. Once an order has shipped, we cannot change the delivery address.
