Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number.

You can track your order by:

  1. Visiting our Track Order page
  2. Logging into your account and viewing your order history
  3. Contacting our customer service team

Returns Policy

Return Eligibility

You may return most new, unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

Items must be returned in their original packaging and in the same condition as when you received them.

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Intimate apparel
  • Swimwear
  • Sale items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Gift cards

Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account and go to your order history
  2. Select the order containing the item(s) you wish to return
  3. Click on "Return Items" and follow the instructions
  4. Print the return label and attach it to your package
  5. Drop off the package at any authorized shipping location

You can also initiate a return by contacting our customer service team.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you about the status of your refund.

If approved, your refund will be processed and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 5-7 business days.

Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for a different size or color, please return the original item and place a new order for the desired item.

This ensures the fastest processing time and availability of your preferred item.
