Tracking Information

At BrandStore, we understand the excitement of waiting for your new purchase to arrive. We provide detailed tracking information to keep you informed every step of the way. Here's what you need to know about tracking your order:

When Will I Receive Tracking Information?

You'll receive an email with tracking information as soon as your order ships. The timing depends on your purchase:

  • In-stock items: Tracking information is typically sent within 1-2 business days after your order is placed
  • Made-to-order items: Tracking is sent after production is complete (usually 5-7 business days)
  • Pre-order items: Tracking is sent once the item becomes available and ships
  • Mixed orders: You may receive multiple tracking numbers if items ship separately

Understanding Your Tracking Status

Your order will go through several stages as it makes its way to you:

  • Order Placed: We've received your order and payment is being processed
  • Payment Confirmed: Your payment has been verified and accepted
  • Order Processing: We're preparing your items for shipment
  • Order Shipped: Your package has left our warehouse
  • In Transit: Your package is on the way to your delivery address
  • Out for Delivery: Your package is on a delivery vehicle for final delivery
  • Delivered: Your package has been delivered to the specified address

Please note that once your package is shipped, the carrier handles delivery and updates the tracking information. There may be a short delay between actual delivery and tracking updates.

Common Tracking Questions

Why hasn't my tracking updated?

Tracking information may not update for 24-48 hours after shipping. During busy periods or holidays, updates may be delayed. If your tracking hasn't updated for more than 3 days, please contact us.

My order shows "delivered," but I haven't received it. What should I do?

If your order shows as delivered but you haven't received it:

  1. Check with other household members who may have accepted the package
  2. Look around your property, including porches, side doors, and garages
  3. Check with neighbors who might have received it
  4. Wait 24 hours, as carriers sometimes mark packages as delivered shortly before actual delivery
  5. If you still can't locate your package, contact our customer service team
